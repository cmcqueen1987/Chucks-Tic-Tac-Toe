Chuck's Tic Tac Toe!

Tic Tac Toe, one of the oldest games in human history, originated from the ancient Roman Empire around the first century BCE and it was called "Terni Lapilli". Tic-tac-toe is a two player game that takes turn marking spaces on a 3 by 3 grid and the objective of the game is to place three connecting marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row. Grab a friend and give it a try!
